About Us

We are billing company that brings over 20 years experience, dedicated to serve our clients efficiently and thoroughly to your satisfaction. Our customers enjoy increased net collections and reductions in Accounts Receivable. Prime Billing both develops and uses medical billing technology. Clients automatically get a rapid response to industry changes and Prime Billing staff has the tools to deal with each client’s specific needs.

By outsourcing your medical billing services to Prime Billing, you can improve your reimbursement rates by:

  • More accurate coding
  • Under & over coding
  • Patient collections
  • Improved capturing of charge
  • Improved eligibility rate
  • Daily industry updates
  • MRA & HETIS scoring
  • PQRS reporting
  • Nonexistent aging

What Prime Billing can do?

Our approach towards medical billing and reimbursement is comprehensive with practical and innovative design to meet the demands of the changing healthcare economy. With Prime Billing you receive:.

  • Eligibility Verification
  • CPT/ICD-10 Coding
  • Electronic Remittance & Payment Posting
  • Claim Submission
  • Denial Management
  • Accounts Payable Services
  • Accounts Receivable Follow-Up
  • Physician Credentialing
  • Patient Billing & Collections
  • Business Intelligence & A/R Analytics
  • Customized Reporting
  • Call Center Patient Enquiry and Follow-up
